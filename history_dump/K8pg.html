About Me

I'm a 3D artist and real estate visualization specialist with a passion for creating immersive digital experiences. With a background from Glendon and experience at 7TV, I've developed expertise in architectural rendering, VR environment design, and interactive property walkthroughs.

My vision is to help real estate professionals, architects, and creative agencies bring their projects to life through cutting-edge 3D visualization and VR technology. I believe in creating compelling visuals that not only showcase projects but tell their story.

What I Offer

Real Estate Renders

Interior & exterior 3D visualization that helps buyers envision properties and architects refine their designs.

VR Tours

Interactive property walkthroughs that let clients explore spaces in immersive virtual reality environments.

3D Animation

Motion graphics and character design for marketing campaigns, presentations, and creative projects.
