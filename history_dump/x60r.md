## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Where do I put my images?**
A: `/projects/` folder (next to index.html)

**Q: What size should images be?**
A: 1200x800px minimum, under 500KB

**Q: What if I have 10 projects?**
A: Update `index.html` - duplicate project items and set categories

**Q: Can I change colors?**
A: Yes! Edit `:root` in `styles.css`

**Q: Can I remove the category filters?**
A: Yes! Delete the filter buttons in `index.html`
